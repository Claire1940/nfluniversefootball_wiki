### Wide Receiver IQ: The "Wiggly" Technique
The "Wiggly" is a movement exploit where the receiver rapidly alternates directions while conserving stamina. This confuses the defender's tracking, creating a "burn" effect where the receiver gains a massive speed advantage. Combined with the use of WR pads on the field, which provide a temporary velocity boost, an intelligent receiver can become unguardable.
