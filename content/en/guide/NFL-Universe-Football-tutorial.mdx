<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Controls and Gameplay Mechanics">

Success on the gridiron requires precise execution. This **NFL Universe Football tutorial** breaks down the controls for both PC and Mobile players. Unlike many Roblox sports games, timing in NFL Universe Football is sensitive to server latency and player positioning. You cannot simply "spam" the catch button; you must face the ball and time the prompt.

| Action | PC Input | Mobile Input | Key Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Move** | WASD | Left Joystick | Use angles to outmaneuver defenders |
| **Sprint** | Shift | Sprint Button | Save stamina for the final 20 yards |
| **Throw Pass** | Hold/Release Left Click | Tap/Hold Throw Button | Release toward the route, not the player |
| **Catch/Intersept** | Left Click | Catch Button | Face the ball and time the icon prompt |
| **Dive/Tackle** | E | Dive Button | Wait for the runner to commit to a lane |
| **Jump** | Spacebar | Jump Button | Only jump when the ball is at its peak |
| **Kick/FG** | Meter Prompts | Screen Taps | Focus on the green zone for accuracy |

<Callout type="warning" title="Common Mistake">
Avoid "hero ball" sprinting. Constant sprinting makes your routes sloppy and significantly increases the chance of missing a tackle. Use bursts of speed only when creating separation or closing a gap.
</Callout>

### Advanced Passing and Reading the Field
As a Quarterback, your "reads" determine the outcome of the drive. Do not stare down a single receiver. Instead, scan the field for open zones. If the defense is playing deep coverage, look for short "slant" or "drag" routes. If they are blitzing, a quick "bullet pass" (short hold on the mouse/tap) can beat the pressure.
