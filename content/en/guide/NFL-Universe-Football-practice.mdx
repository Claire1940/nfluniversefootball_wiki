<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Maximizing XP Multipliers for Practice Sessions">

To make your practice sessions as efficient as possible, you should stack active XP multipliers. Relying solely on base XP gains makes the grind to 100 OVR significantly longer, especially as the XP requirements increase at higher levels. Fortunately, there are several free and premium multipliers available to players.

The most notable free multiplier is the Double XP Weekend. This event occurs every other weekend, alternating with Double Coins Weekends. It begins every Saturday morning at 7:00 AM UTC (3:00 AM EST) and runs for exactly 48 hours, concluding on Monday morning. This is the prime time to schedule your heavy practice sessions.

| Multiplier Source | XP Boost | Cost | Availability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Double XP Weekend** | +100% | Free | Every other weekend |
| **Competitive Play** | +15% | Free | Always active |
| **Amethyst Subscription** | +25% | $5.00 per month | Always active |
| **Diamond Subscription** | +50% | $10.00 per month | Always active |
| **Tiered Offers** | +100% (15 mins) | Robux | Dynamic |

By combining a Diamond subscription, a active Double XP Weekend, competitive play, and temporary tiered boosts, players can theoretically reach an XP multiplier of up to 290%. Even without premium subscriptions, utilizing the free competitive bonus and weekend events will drastically shorten your path to 100 OVR.

<Callout type="tip" title="Multiplier Stacking">

<Section icon={Swords} title="The Ultimate NFL Universe Football Practice Drill">

While playing standard games in the Park or Trade Hub can be entertaining, it is not the fastest way to gain XP. The absolute best method for rapid progression is a specialized **NFL Universe Football practice** drill utilizing 2v2 matchmaking. 

This method eliminates the unpredictable nature of public lobbies and ensures you consistently execute positive plays. By setting up a controlled environment, you can secure high-yield XP actions without the risk of turnovers or defensive interference.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Set Up Your Squad">
    Log into your main account on your primary gaming device and create a matchmaking squad.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Connect an Alternate Account">
    Log into an alternate account on a secondary device, such as an old phone or tablet. Send an invite from your main account to this alternate account to join your matchmaking squad.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Queue for 2v2 Matchmaking">
    Initiate a 2v2 matchmaking queue. Because the matchmaking pool is random, you will quickly find a game, often against less experienced players or idle opponents.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Deep Bomb Drill">
    Once in the game, ignore the alternate account's controls. On your main account, consistently throw deep passes (bombs) and user-catch them. This cycle secures massive XP.
  </Step>
</Steps>

This drill works because matchmaking offers incredibly fast transition times. Win or lose, you can find and enter a new match within 10 seconds. This high frequency of play, combined with the lack of highly competitive defense, makes it vastly superior to public Park courts.

| In-Game Action | XP Impact | Practice Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Passing Completion** | Positive | High |
| **Reception** | Positive | High |
| **Touchdown** | Positive | Medium |
| **Tackle / Sack** | Positive | Low |
| **Interception Thrown** | Negative | Avoid |
| **Getting Sacked** | Negative | Avoid |

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch the Defense">
Even in casual matchmaking, do not force passes into heavy coverage. Throwing an interception will deduct XP, which actively works against your practice goals. Play smart and prioritize clean completions.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Weekly Goals and Progression Milestones">

To supplement your on-field practice, you must actively complete your Weekly Goals. Each week, the game provides a progression path consisting of 12 distinct challenges. Completing these challenges rewards you with XP, coins, and ultimately, a Weekly Pack.

You can hold up to 8 active goals at a time, and the system generates 2 new goals every 3 hours. The difficulty of these goals scales dynamically based on your current OVR. Lower-level players receive straightforward tasks, while high-OVR players must complete more demanding statistical milestones.

| Completed Goals | XP Reward | Coin Reward | Bonus Item |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **3 Goals** | Low | Low | None |
| **6 Goals** | Medium | Medium | None |
| **9 Goals** | High | High | None |
| **12 Goals** | Maximum | Maximum | Weekly Pack |

Completing milestones at 3, 6, and 9 goals provides massive lump sums of XP that can instantly boost your player by up to 10 levels at a time. Integrating these goals into your daily practice routine is essential for keeping pace with the seasonal reset.
