<Section icon={Shield} title="Support Tiers: The Specialists (RB, Kicker, Blocker)">

While the playmakers grab the headlines, the specialists fill the gaps in the **NFL Universe Football position priority** matrix. These roles are often situational but can be the deciding factor in close games, especially when the clock is winding down or the weather conditions in the 32 official NFL stadiums affect passing accuracy.

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Rushing Obsession">

The Blocker is the lowest priority in terms of individual glory but remains a tactical necessity. In 6v6, having at least one dedicated player focused on the line of scrimmage can provide the extra two seconds a QB needs to find an open man.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Choose Your Starting Position">

Choosing your first role should be based on your comfort with Roblox controls and your understanding of football fundamentals. Following a structured path will help you build your OVR faster and avoid the frustration of high-pressure roles before you are ready.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start as a Wide Receiver">
    Begin your journey by learning how to catch. Focus on positioning yourself under the ball and timing your clicks. This teaches you how the ball moves in the game without the pressure of throwing it.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Switch to Defensive Back">
    Once you understand how receivers move, play as a DB to learn how to counter them. Staying between the receiver and the end zone is the most important defensive skill you can acquire.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Practice in the Hub">
    Use the dedicated Practice Mode to work on your throwing accuracy. Test different power levels and release angles until you can hit a moving target consistently.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Take the QB Mantle">
    After mastering catching and defending, step into the Quarterback role. Start in casual 6v6 matches before moving into the high-stakes environment of Ranked Park.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="OVR and Progression">

</Callout>
