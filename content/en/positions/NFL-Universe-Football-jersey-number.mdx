<Section icon={Target} title="How to Change Your Jersey Number">

Updating your player profile is a straightforward process, but navigating the user interface after the winter update can sometimes confuse newer players. Securing your favorite digit before hopping into your next public match is crucial for maintaining your team's visual cohesion.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Customization Menu">
    From the main lobby of NFL Universe Football, locate and click on the **Customize** or **Locker Room** tab on the left side of your screen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Your Player Profile">
    Choose the specific player slot you want to modify. You can have multiple profiles saved for different positions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Edit Jersey Details">
    Locate the **Jersey Number** text field. Click on it and input your desired number ranging from 0 to 99.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Save and Apply Changes">
    Click the **Apply** or **Save** button to confirm your changes. Your new number will now display on your uniform in all active matches.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If your changes do not appear immediately in-game, it is typically due to server-side synchronization delays. Simply re-entering the locker room or joining a new server will force the game to load your updated profile. Additionally, some premium jersey fonts and custom team cosmetics may require in-game coins or Robux to unlock, but changing your basic jersey number remains entirely free for all players.

<Callout type="warning" title="Duplicate Number Restrictions">
While public servers allow multiple players to wear the same number, competitive private leagues often enforce strict unique-number rules. Keep a secondary number in mind.
</Callout>
