<Section icon={Target} title="OVR Thresholds and Attribute Requirements">

To consistently perform an **NFL Universe Football one handed catch**, you cannot rely on a base character. <Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Guide to the One-Handed Snag">

Mastering the **NFL Universe Football one handed catch** requires a blend of positional awareness and button discipline. Follow these steps to improve your success rate in both 6v6 matches and Ranked Park sessions.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Create Separation">
    Use your route running to get away from the Defensive Back. The best one-handed catches happen when you have a small window of open space, allowing the animation to play out without defensive interference.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Track the Ball Trajectory">
    Watch the ball as soon as it leaves the Quarterback's hands. In NFL Universe Football, the ball has a visible arc. Position yourself so you are running *through* the point where the ball will be at its peak or just beginning its descent.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The High-Point Jump">
    Press the **Spacebar** (PC) or **Jump** (Mobile) just before the ball reaches you. You want to be at the highest point of your jump when you initiate the catch. This triggers the vertical one-handed animation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Timed Input">
    Click the **Left Mouse Button** or tap the **Catch** icon exactly when the ball enters your player's reach. Do not spam the button; a single, well-timed press is more effective for triggering elite animations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success Tip">
If the ball is thrown low or away from you, try combining the catch input with a dive (**E** on PC). This often results in a spectacular one-handed diving catch that can save a broken play.
</Callout>

</Section>
