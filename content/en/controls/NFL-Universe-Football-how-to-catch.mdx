<Section icon={Shield} title="Defensive Interference and Catch Security">

Defenders in **NFL Universe Football** are designed to disrupt your timing. If a defender uses the "D tackle" or "Dive" mechanic while the ball is in the air, it can jar the ball loose. This is known as a "Pass Breakup" (PBU). To counter this, you must focus on **Catch Security**.

| Factor | Impact on Catching | Countermeasure |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Defender Proximity** | Increases drop chance | Use "Shielding" movement to block the defender. |
| **Ball Velocity** | Harder to time | Practice against high-speed QB builds. |
| **Weather (if active)** | Slippery ball | Focus on Standard catches over flashy ones. |
| **Player Stamina** | Slows animations | Don't sprint for the entire duration of the play. |

**Receiver Attributes to Prioritize:**
- **Hands**: Directly increases the catch success percentage.
- **Speed**: Helps in creating the necessary separation from elite DBs.
- **Jump**: Essential for "High-Pointing" the ball in the end zone.
- **Release**: Helps you get off the line of scrimmage without being jammed.
