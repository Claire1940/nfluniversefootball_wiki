<Section icon={Target} title="Tackling Techniques and Timing Strategies">

Mastering the tackle window is the most difficult aspect of defense for beginners. Many players make the mistake of "panic diving," where they press the tackle button while still five yards away from the runner. This results in your character falling to the turf, leaving the runner with a clear path to the end zone.

To improve your success rate, you must distinguish between a wrap-up tackle and a dive. A wrap-up tackle occurs when you are in direct contact with the player, while a dive is used to close the final gap. In the 2026 meta, the "Hit Stick" mechanic (timing your hit at the moment of impact) is essential for forcing fumbles in Ranked Park.

| Tackle Method | Success Rate | Risk Level | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Wrap-up** | High | Low | Goal line stands |
| **Lunge Dive** | Medium | High | Open field pursuit |
| **Strip Ball** | Low | Very High | Behind by multiple scores |
| **Gang Tackle** | Very High | Low | Stopping powerful RBs |

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Over-Committing">
If you miss a dive in NFL Universe Football, your player will undergo a recovery animation. During this 1-2 second window, you are completely out of the play. Only dive if it is your last resort.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step: Executing the Perfect Stop">

If you want to stop every drive, follow this systematic approach to defensive play. Consistency is more important than the occasional highlight-reel interception.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Pre-Snap Alignment">
    Identify the offensive formation. If they have three receivers on one side, shift your defense to cover the "strong side" of the field.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The First Step">
    At the snap, drop back 2-3 yards to avoid being burned by a quick streak route. Maintain a cushion between you and the receiver.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Closing the Gap">
    Once the ball is thrown or handed off, use your sprint (Shift) to close the distance. Do not use your tackle button yet.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Contact Phase">
    When you are within the tackle box (approx. 1.5 yards), press the **E key**. If the runner jukes, use your strafe to mirror their movement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Finish">
    Hold the tackle input until the "Tackled" notification appears on your screen. This ensures the play is dead and the whistle blows.
  </Step>
</Steps>
