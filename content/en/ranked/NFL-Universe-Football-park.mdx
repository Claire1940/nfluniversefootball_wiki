<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering Park Controls: PC and Mobile">

Success in the **NFL Universe Football park** depends on your ability to execute precise movements under pressure. The control scheme is designed to be accessible but has a high skill ceiling, particularly when it comes to "reading" the defense and timing your dives.

| Action | PC Input | Mobile Input | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sprint** | Shift | Sprint Button | Creating separation on routes |
| **Throw Pass** | Left Click (Hold/Release) | Throw Button (Hold/Release) | QB delivering the ball to WR |
| **Catch** | Left Click (Timed) | Catch Button (Timed) | Securing the ball mid-air |
| **Dive/Tackle** | E | Dive Button | Stopping a runner or contesting |
| **Block/Handoff**| X | On-screen Prompt | Supporting the ball carrier |
| **Jump** | Spacebar | Jump Button | Contesting high-point passes |

<Callout type="success" title="Success Tip">
For Quarterbacks on PC, the strength of your pass is determined by how long you hold Left Click. A quick tap results in a "lob" pass, while a long hold creates a "bullet" pass. Use lobs for deep routes and bullets for quick slants.
</Callout>
