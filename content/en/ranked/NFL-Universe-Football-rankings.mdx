<Section icon={Settings} title="Mastering Controls for Higher Rankings">

To climb the **NFL Universe Football rankings**, you must move beyond basic movement and master the technical nuances of passing, catching, and tackling. The game supports PC, Mobile, and Controller inputs, each with specific advantages.

| Action | PC Input | Mobile Input | Pro Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Throw Pass** | Hold/Release Left Click | Tap/Hold Throw Button | Release toward the route, not the player |
| **Catch Ball** | Left Click (Timed) | Catch Button (Timed) | Face the ball to increase success rate |
| **Dive/Tackle** | Key E | Dive Button | Wait for the runner to commit to a lane |
| **Sprint** | Shift | Sprint Button | Use in bursts to preserve stamina |
| **Jump** | Spacebar | Jump Button | Only jump when the ball is at its apex |

<Callout type="warning" title="Common Mistake">
Avoid "panic jumping" or "panic catching." The game's engine rewards precise timing. Clicking the catch button too early often results in a dropped ball, even if your positioning is perfect.
</Callout>

Successful players focus on **Route Reading**. As a QB, you must anticipate where your Receiver will be in two seconds, rather than where they are now. As a Defender, your goal is to stay between the Receiver and the End Zone, a tactic known as "Containment."

</Section>
