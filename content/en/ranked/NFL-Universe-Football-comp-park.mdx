To succeed in the **NFL Universe Football comp park**, you must master the nuanced controls that separate casual players from pro-tier competitors. The competitive environment rewards "reads"—the ability to predict defender movement and throw into open windows.

| Action | PC Input | Mobile Input | Competitive Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Precision Pass** | Hold/Release Left Click | Tap/Hold Throw Button | Release at 80% power for medium routes |
| **Aggressive Catch** | Left Click (Timed) | Catch Button (Timed) | Face the ball to increase catch success |
| **Defensive Dive** | E Key | Dive Button | Only dive when the runner commits to a lane |
| **Sprint Burst** | Shift Key | Sprint Toggle | Save stamina for the final 10 yards of a route |
| **Juke/Move** | WASD Keys | Joystick | Use rapid directional changes to break ankles |
