<Section icon={Shield} title="Essential Controls for Competitive Play">

Mechanical mastery separates average players from Legends. Understanding basic movement is not enough; you must master the exact timing of every competitive action. Use this reference table to map your inputs and execute advanced plays under pressure.

| Action | PC Input | Mobile Input | Ranked Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Throw Pass** | Hold & Release Left Click | Tap & Hold Throw Button | Release to lead your receiver into open space. |
| **Catch Ball** | Left Click on arrival | Tap Catch on arrival | Face the ball and time your click at its peak. |
| **Sprint** | Shift | Drag Joystick / Sprint Button | Use in short bursts to preserve stamina. |
| **Dive / Tackle** | E | Dive Button | Wait for the runner to commit before diving. |
| **Jump** | Spacebar | Jump Button | Use to contest high passes or block throws. |
| **Block / Handoff** | X | On-Screen Prompt | Support your ball carrier or execute designed runs. |

When playing defense, avoid the temptation to spam the dive tackle button. Missed tackles grant the offensive player free yards and open lanes. Instead, shadow the ball carrier, match their angles, and execute your tackle only when they are within guaranteed range.

<Callout type="info" title="Mastering the Catch Meter">
Do not rely on automatic catching. Manually positioning your player and timing your catch input right as the ball enters your character's hands drastically reduces dropped passes and increases your chances of securing contested catches.
</Callout>
