<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Gameplay Mechanics and Controls">

Mastering the controls is the first step toward climbing the **NFL Universe Football ranked** ladder. The game supports PC, Mobile, and Controller inputs, though most competitive players prefer PC for the precision of mouse-based passing and keyboard-driven movement. The mechanics are built around "reads"—the ability to see the defense's positioning and react instantly.

### PC and Mobile Control Scheme

| Action | PC Input | Mobile Input | Tactical Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Move** | **WASD** | **Left Joystick** | Basic positioning and route running |
| **Sprint** | **Shift** | **Sprint Button** | Closing gaps or creating separation |
| **Throw Pass** | **Hold/Release Left Click** | **Tap/Hold Button** | Quarterback delivery to receivers |
| **Catch** | **Left Click (Timed)** | **Catch Button** | Securing the ball or interceptions |
| **Dive/Tackle** | **E** | **Tackle Button** | Stopping the ball carrier or contesting |
| **Jump** | **Spacebar** | **Jump Button** | High-pointing the ball during catches |
| **Block/Handoff** | **X** | **On-screen Prompt** | Supporting the run game or protection |

On defense, the "E" key for diving is your most powerful tool, but it is also the most dangerous. A missed dive leaves your defender on the ground, giving the receiver a clear path to the end zone. Competitive play rewards patience; stay between the receiver and the goal line, and only commit to the tackle once the ball is in the air or the runner has committed to a lane.

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Avoid "ball hawking" (chasing the ball blindly). In ranked 6v6 matches, disciplined zone or man-to-man coverage is always more effective than three defenders sprinting toward a single receiver, leaving the rest of the field open.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="OVR Progression and Coin Farming">

Progression in **NFL Universe Football ranked** is tied to your Overall Rating (OVR). This numerical value increases as you perform successful actions in matches—catches, tackles, touchdowns, and completions. Alongside OVR, you earn Coins, which are used for Pro Packs, team-branded merch, and cosmetic upgrades.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Join the Official Group">
    Before playing your first match, join the **NFL Universe Football by Voldex** Roblox group. This grants a permanent **25% Coin Bonus** on all match earnings.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Complete Casual Matches">
    Play your first three casual matches. Focus on high-percentage plays (short passes and safe tackles) to begin building your baseline OVR.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enter Ranked Park">
    Once the ranked queue unlocks, focus on a single role. Specializing in one position (like DB or WR) allows you to master specific mechanics faster than switching every game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Premium Bonuses">
    If you have Roblox Premium, you receive an additional **25% Coin Bonus**. Combined with the group bonus, this significantly accelerates your ability to buy Pro Packs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Trade Hub Participation">
    Use the Trade Hub to exchange duplicate gear or rare NFL items. This is the fastest way to complete specific team sets without relying solely on pack luck.
  </Step>
</Steps>
