Success in NFL Universe Football is determined by your ability to translate your football IQ into button presses. The 2026 update has refined the input latency for mobile players, making the virtual joystick much more responsive during "Park" matches. However, PC players still benefit from the precision of mouse-based passing.

| Action | PC Input | Mobile Input | Pro Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Move** | WASD | Left Joystick | Use small taps for precise route adjustments |
| **Sprint** | Shift | Sprint Button | Save stamina for the final 20 yards |
| **Throw Pass** | Hold/Release Click | Tap/Hold Button | Release early for lobs, hold for bullets |
| **Catch** | Left Click | Catch Button | Face the ball and time the click at the apex |
| **Dive/Tackle** | E Key | Dive Button | Only dive when the runner commits to a lane |
| **Jump** | Spacebar | Jump Button | Don't spam; jumping slows your horizontal speed |
| **Switch Player** | Mouse Scroll | On-screen Toggle | Essential for defense to track the ball carrier |
