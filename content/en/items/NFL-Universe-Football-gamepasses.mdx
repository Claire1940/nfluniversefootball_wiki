<Section icon={Map} title="Mastering the Controls: Offense and Defense">

To make the most of your gear and bonuses, you must master the mechanics. NFL Universe Football uses a precise input system that rewards timing over button-mashing. Whether you are on PC or Mobile, understanding the "Throw Pass" and "Dive" mechanics is the difference between a touchdown and a turnover.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Movement and Positioning">
    Use WASD (PC) or the Joystick (Mobile) to find open space. Avoid sprinting constantly; save your stamina for the "Shift" burst when the ball is in the air or you are chasing a carrier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Passing Game">
    As a QB, hold and release Left Click to control pass power. As a WR, time your Jump (Spacebar) and Catch (Left Click) only when the ball arrives to avoid "early jump" penalties.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defensive Pressure">
    Use the "E" key to dive or tackle. Do not commit too early; wait for the runner to enter a lane before lunging. Stay between the receiver and the end zone at all times.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Practice Mode">
